HEALTH SCIENCE

About Our topic

We all are familiar with the term “Health is Wealth”. In other words, to lead a healthy life, one
must be fit and fine both physically and mentally. Good health is a secret of every happy person.
Staying healthy for children is vital for proper growth and development of mind and body as
they need to focus in studies and participate in other physical activities.

We all are aware about the current pandemic i.e. Covid 19. SARS-CoV-2, the virus causes
coronavirus disease 19 (COVID-19). Billions of people are being affected and millions of
people are being infected. We can’t go outside and play, school is closed and we can’t visit
family and friends. COVID-19 has caused many people to be anxious, stressed, lonely and
possibly depressed.

In response to this global health crisis, researchers are working on developing an effective
coronavirus vaccine. Researchers are also trying to find out possible medicine to treat affected
people. Unless the invention of new vaccine, we will have to accept new normal in the current
situation. People can protect themselves and others by following government guidelines. This
includes wearing a mask, maintaining social distance, wearing gloves and maintaining proper hygiene. We can
protect others by protecting ourselves.

In our current project we are assessing the knowledge of general population about Covid 19,
its preventive measures, and preference of medication through online survey. We are
developing a website dedicated for Covid-19 where we’ll post survey results and related
information. We’ll also create a campaign for awareness generation and subsequently measure
the impact after visiting the site i.e. post exposure to the campaign. We’ll promote the site
through social media channels by taking support from our parents.
